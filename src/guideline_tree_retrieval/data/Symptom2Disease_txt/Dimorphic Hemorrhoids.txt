Does the patient have any of the following?
• Melena, dark red blood per rectum, or postural vital sign abnormalities
• Systemic symptoms (eg, night sweats, fever, weight loss)
• Abdominal pain
• Change in the stool frequency, caliber, or consistency of stools
• Painful defecation
• Evidence of anemia on laboratory testing
