Patient with a clinical diagnosis of seborrheic dermatitis of the scalp/hairy areas

Assess severity

if Mild:
	action:
	Antifungal shampoo (e.g, ketoconazole 2% shampoo) 2 to 3 times per week (refer to Inset). Assess at 4 weeks. If improvement, continue antifungal shampoo once or twice weekly to prevent relapse. Else jump to moderate and severe.
elif Moderate to severe : 
	action:
	Antifungal shampoo 12 to 3 times per week (refer to Inset) plus High-potency topical corticosteroid(foam, lotion, spray, or shampoo) once daily until symptoms subside orup to 4 weeks. Assess at 4 weeks. 
	If improvement:
		 continue antifungal shampoo once or twice weekly to prevent relapse.
	else if no improvement:
		Trial of systemic antifungal(e.g, itraconazole 200 mg orallyonce daily for 7 days). Assess at 4 weeks. If improvement, continue antifungal shampoo once or twice weekly to prevent relapse. Else, if no improvement, reconsider diagnosis.
